## ğŸ” Key Analysis & Findings
Based on the data profiling conducted in the notebook:

### 1. The "Pareto" Effect in Restaurants
A small segment of restaurant partners drives the majority of the platform's volume. **Shake Shack, The Meatball Shop, Blue Ribbon Sushi, and Blue Ribbon Fried Chicken** are the core pillars of the current business model.

### 2. Operational Latency
Analysis revealed that weekend delivery times are significantly higher than weekdays. This suggests a need for dynamic delivery partner allocation to maintain SLAs.

### 3. Revenue vs. Satisfaction
- **American and Japanese** cuisines lead in volume.
- **French Cuisine** represents a "Premium" opportunity: it has high costs but consistently high customer satisfaction, making it ideal for luxury branding.
